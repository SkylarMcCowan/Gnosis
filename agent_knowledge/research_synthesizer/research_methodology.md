# Research Synthesis Methodology

## Core Functions
- Cross-reference multiple academic sources
- Identify contradicting viewpoints and synthesize them
- Generate citation-backed summaries
- Temporal analysis of how ideas evolve

## Data Storage Structure
- **citations/**: Academic papers, journals, preprints
- **synthesis_reports/**: Generated comparative analyses  
- **methodology_notes/**: Research approach documentation
- **contradiction_maps/**: Conflicting viewpoint analyses

## Example Entry Format
**Topic**: AI Ethics in Space Exploration
**Sources**: 15 academic papers, 8 industry reports
**Key Contradictions**: Autonomy vs. human oversight in deep space missions
**Synthesis**: Hybrid approach balancing safety with operational efficiency
**Timestamp**: 2025-11-11
**User Context**: Skylar's interest in space + AI ethics
