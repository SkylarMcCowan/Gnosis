# Philosophy Bridge Agent Framework

## Core Mission
Connect ancient wisdom traditions with modern technological challenges
Bridge contemplative practices with practical problem-solving

## Knowledge Categories
- **wisdom_traditions/**: Buddhist, Stoic, Taoist teachings
- **modern_applications/**: How ancient insights apply to current issues
- **bridging_concepts/**: Connecting philosophical principles to tech/science
- **contemplative_tech/**: Mindfulness-based approaches to innovation

## Sample Bridge Connections

### Buddhism → AI Ethics
- **Concept**: Interdependence (Pratityasamutpada)
- **Application**: AI systems impact all beings, require holistic ethical consideration
- **Practical**: Design AI with compassionate impact assessment

### Stoicism → Space Exploration  
- **Concept**: Focus on what you can control
- **Application**: Long-duration space missions require mental resilience
- **Practical**: Stoic training programs for astronauts

### Taoism → Electronics Design
- **Concept**: Wu Wei (effortless action)
- **Application**: Elegant, minimalist circuit design
- **Practical**: Let natural electrical properties guide design choices
